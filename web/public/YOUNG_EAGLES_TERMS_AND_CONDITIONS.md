## 6. CANCELLATION & WITHDRAWAL

### 6.1 Notice Period
**30 days written notice** is required when withdrawing a child from the school. 

Failure to provide proper notice will render the parent/guardian liable for school fees for the month following withdrawal.

### 6.2 4th Term Policy (October - December)
- **No cancellations or withdrawals will be accepted during the 4th term**
- If a child is withdrawn at the end of the academic year, the parent/guardian remains liable to pay for the **complete 4th term** fees

### 6.3 Medical Concession
If a child needs to stay away from school for medical reasons:
- **Half (50%) of the monthly school fee** is payable to keep the child's space reserved
- This fee is **non-refundable**
- To qualify: Submit a **written request with a doctor's letter** clearly indicating the dates of absence

### 6.4 School-Initiated Termination
Young Eagles is entitled to terminate the enrolment of any child:

- Summarily and with immediate effect, if the child is guilty of an offense which, in the sole opinion of the school, renders their continued enrolment impossible
- In such cases, the Account Holder will be refunded a pro-rata proportion of fees already paid in advance (after deduction of any amounts owing to the school)

## 7. SCHOOL RULES & POLICIES

### 7.1 School Calendar
Young Eagles operates in conjunction with the Department of Education's terms and holidays. As a Christian school, certain Christian holidays are observed.

### 7.2 Enrolment Requirements
All parents must:
- Complete an **Enrolment Form** and all other relevant forms
- Pay a **Placement Fee** on enrolment
- Provide copies of:
  - Child's birth certificate
  - Identity documents or passports of both parents
  - Proof of payment of Placement Fee
  - Proof of payment of 1st month fees
  - Child's most recent school report (if applicable)

### 7.3 School Hours
**Operating Hours:** 06:00 – 18:00 (Monday to Friday)  
**School Day:** 08:00 – 16:00  
**Grace Period:** 30 minutes after home time (before aftercare charges apply)

### 7.4 Aftercare Services
Children not collected within 30 minutes of home time will automatically be placed in aftercare for safety reasons.

**Aftercare Fees:**
- **R300.00 per month** (regular aftercare)
- **R75.00 per day** (ad-hoc aftercare)

Aftercare operates from the end of the school day until 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

### 7.5 Attendance & Absence
- Parents must notify the office or teacher of any absence **before 9:00 AM** (phone call or SMS)
- Children suffering from colds, coughs, or infectious conditions should be kept home until recovered
- Medication to be administered at school requires **written and signed directive** in the child's notebook

### 7.6 Clothing & Personal Items
- All clothing must be **clearly marked** with the child's name
- All children must bring a **marked hat** for outdoor play
- Pack **spare clothing** (marked) for accidents or water play
- **Do not bring:** toys, junk food, yogurt, candy, chocolate, candy bars, or sweet muffins

### 7.7 Class Placement
Children will be placed in **age-appropriate classes** according to their developmental stage and school readiness.

### 7.8 Outings & Excursions
The school organizes outings at least once per term for older classes. A small cost may be required for these activities.

### 7.9 Transport
- The school is **not responsible** for transporting children to or from school
- If parents privately arrange lifts with staff members, the school will **not be held liable** for anything happening to the child during transport

### 7.10 Safety & Security
- Parents must deliver children **personally to the teacher** in charge
- Ensure all gates are **closed** during entrance and exit
- **No child** is allowed to press buttons to open the main gate
- Notify the school immediately of any changes to:
  - Person collecting or dropping off your child
  - Telephone numbers
  - Physical address
